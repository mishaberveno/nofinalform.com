No Final Form

I started No Final Form as a work-in-progress newsletter for sharing updates on the side projects I’m working on.

Welcome to No Final Form — a newsletter from Misha Berveno about the power of words in branding and beyond. We cover the business of writing and tangentially related things to help you write better.

No Final Form is about explaining the core of ideas you rarely see published online, assisted with examples from history and my own experience. The heuristic used for selecting topics aims to follow the Lindy effect: Will this be valuable in 5 years?
