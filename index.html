No Final Form

If you’ve ever wanted to see what I’m up to, this is the place.

No Final Form is an ever-evolving newsletter for sharing updates and takeaways from my side projects. For now, it focuses on short stories and web app launches. But if I ever win an Oscar for Best Original Screenplay, you’ll find out about it here too.

I don’t plan to turn No Final Form into a content machine. Expect an email every few months at most, announcing a new project and breaking it down into reflections on writing, design and programming.
